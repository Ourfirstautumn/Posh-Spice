This means I’ve successfully managed to separate the content and presentation.

Therefore, I’ve come up with a system of breaking up each of my pages into parts. I put the components in the HTML then proceed to alter EVERYTHING about them in the CSS. Height, width, position, background, et cetera. Each part has its own ID which helps me easily find it in the CSS.

I’m honestly not sure where the shortcomings are in my markup. I guess the biggest weakness is, as I said earlier, if the CSS isn’t functioning I’m done for. I don’t know how I can change that though, seeing as I’m supposed to have presentation and content separate.
